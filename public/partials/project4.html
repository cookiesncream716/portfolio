Discussion Board was an assignment I completed while learning the full MEAN stack. It is a simple discussion forum app that allows a user to post a topic or question based on pre-defined categories. Users may then post their answers to other user questions and also comment on the answers. In additon, they can vote answers up or down. A profile page displays how many topics, answers, and comments each user has posted.

TECHNOLOGIES

JavaScript, node.js, Mongo, mongoose.db,Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3
