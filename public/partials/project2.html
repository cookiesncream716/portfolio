Hungry Belly was designed, built and deployed with 2 other developers after completing the Ruby on Rails stack. It grants users the ability to choose guests to invite to dinner; a map is then shown displaying 5 restaurants to meet that or centrally located according to the addresses of everyone invited. Once the user selects a restaurant, the user can send a text inviting all attendees, including the date, time, and location of the dinner date.

TECHNOLOGIES

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Yelp API, Geoloction API, Google Maps API, Twilio API, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3
