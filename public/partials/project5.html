PairCoding was designed and created by a team of 2 devolopers and then deployed in just 2 days. This website allows users to find fellow programmers in the immediate area working with a particular language; users are then able to connect and code together.

TECHNOLOGIES

Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Geoloction API, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3, AWS
